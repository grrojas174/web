Position estimation pipeline for associative and non-associative processes

Pose estimation has become more of an important topic in the behavioral sciences. Pose estimation can be a powerful tool for identifying behavioral states or sequences involved with consumption of natural or drug rewards. My interest is in identifying behavioral patterns that emerge with both pharmacological and optogenetic manipulations.
